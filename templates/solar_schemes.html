Government Solar Panel Subsidy Schemes in the UK

Smart Export Guarantee (SEG)

The SEG allows households to earn money for exporting surplus solar energy back to the grid.

  • Eligibility: All UK households with solar installations.
  • Payments depend on energy supplier rates.
Learn More
Energy Company Obligation (ECO4)

ECO4 provides funding for energy-saving measures, including solar panel installations, for low-income households.

  • Eligibility: Low-income and vulnerable households.
  • Support varies by energy supplier and location.
Learn More
Renewable Heat Incentive (RHI)

The RHI offers financial incentives for generating renewable energy, including solar thermal systems.

  • Eligibility: Solar thermal systems installed in homes.
  • Payments are made quarterly over 7 years.
